Features

A smart shoe sole empowering unparalleled convinience

01

Real time spatial haptic feedback according to music

Provides real-time spatial haptic sensations synchronized with the music being played. Through precisely calibrated vibrations and tactile sensations, users can feel the rhythm and beats of their favourite music. This immersive experience enhances music enjoyment, turning every step into a dance to the rhythm of the melody. Whether walking down the street or dancing at a party, the shoe sole elevates the auditory and tactile sensation, creating a unique and engaging experience for the wearer.

02

Gesture recognition and control

Enables users to interact with their smartphones or other connected devices through intuitive hand movements. A simple swipe or tap on the shoe sole's surface allows users to access phone controls, answer calls, or skip tracks without the need to reach for their devices. This feature promotes convenience and reduces distractions during daily activities, making it an ideal hands-free solution for on-the-go individuals.

03

Gaming mode

Hapsole offers a Low Latency Gaming Mode with lag less haptic feedback. This specialized mode enhances the gaming experience, providing precise and synchronized haptic sensations in real-time with the game's audio and visuals. The result is an unparalleled level of immersion, making every gaming moment more lifelike and engaging.

04

GPS Tracking and Geofencing

Hapsole incorporates GPS tracking capabilities, empowering guardians or caregivers to monitor the real-time location of loved ones, such as children or elderly individuals. By connecting the shoe sole to their smartphones or other devices, users can stay informed about the whereabouts of their family members, ensuring their safety and security in crowded or unfamiliar environments

To add an extra layer of security, Hapsole supports geofencing technology. Users can set virtual boundaries or safe zones on maps using the shoe sole's connected app. If the wearer enters or exits these predefined areas, an alert is triggered on their device or sent to designated contacts. Geofencing enhances safety by allowing users to monitor loved ones' movements and receive immediate notifications if they stray from designated safe areas

05

Driver Safe Mode and crash detection

Prioritizing road safety, the shoe sole's Driver Safe Mode utilizes advanced sensors to monitor the driver's movements and vital signs. It detects signs of drowsiness and microsleep, common causes of accidents due to driver fatigue. When fatigue is detected, the shoe sole delivers haptic feedback in the form of vibrations or subtle nudges, promoting driver alertness and reducing the risk of accidents caused by fatigue.

The shoe sole is also equipped with an advanced crash detection system that instantly senses abrupt changes in movement, indicating a collision or impact. In such events, the system immediately notifies the driver's pre-configured emergency contacts, potentially saving lives and minimizing the severity of injuries by enabling swift assistance and response.

06

Activity Tracking and Fitness Monitoring with environmental adaptability

Built-in sensors constantly track various health metrics, such as step count, distance travelled, calories burned, and more. This activity tracking feature encourages an active lifestyle, motivating users to set and achieve exercise goals, and supporting personal well-being. Users can monitor their fitness progress and make data-driven decisions to improve their overall health.

Designed to withstand various environmental conditions, the smart shoe sole is weatherproof and durable. It can handle rain, snow, or uneven terrain, ensuring reliability and longevity, making it suitable for various outdoor activities and adventures.
